Alexia Asgari is a creative technologist, innovation strategist, and computational designer whose work explores the evolving relationship between humanity and technology. Through interdisciplinary media, her art engages themes of craft, mechanization, and digitalization. She holds a Master of Design (Technology) from the Harvard Graduate School of Design and a Bachelor of Architecture with a minor in Perceptual Cognition/Neuroscience from Cornell University.
